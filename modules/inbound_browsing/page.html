We are here to help customers financially impacted by Covid-19. Please call us to discuss your personal circumstances and we will review each situation on a case-by-case basis with a view to give you the best support we can. Call us on0800 028 3746to speak to the team.

Since the UK Governments announcement on Monday 16th March, stating companies who have the capability to work from home should, Hodge has taken the decision that all employees will operate remotely until further notice. The health and safety of Hodge’s employees and customers is our top priority and weve made sure all our staff are able to work from home and are working hard to maintain customer service levels.

Even though our office in Cardiff is closed, we are still fully operational and able to offer our customers and brokers support over the phone, by email or via video conferencing facilities so please continue to contact us as usual.

To help us efficiently process your business we would ask that where possible you email all documents to us rather than post them. For savings, please email deposits@hodge.co.uk and for mortgages, please email support@hodge.co.uk
